# Per-Topic Section Format (Layer 3 — reference)

Every topic section follows this exact shape:

```markdown
#### [Topic Title]
*[N sources: outlet names] | [date range] | [snippet-only notes if any]*

**What happened:**
2–3 sentences on the actual news. Inline attribution: "The Commission announced…
(Politico EU). Industry groups responded… (FT)." Claim only what articles confirm.

**Coverage comparison:**

| Source | Frame (3–5 words) | Emphasized | Downplayed or absent |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|----------------------|
| [Outlet] | [e.g. "compliance burden"] | [specific angle] | [what's missing] |

**Framing analysis:**
3–5 observations. For each: name the dimension (Frame Selection, Salience, Source
Selection, Language, Causal, Omission, Proportion), quote the revealing text,
describe the specific choice — not "outlet X leans Y".

**Evidence summary:**
1. [Claim] (Outlet, date)
2. [Claim] (Outlet, date)
```
