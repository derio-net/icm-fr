# Setup Questionnaire (answered once, at setup)

These answers populate `_config/` (Layer 3) and are reused every run. The per-run
topic goes in `brief.md`, not here — configure the factory, not the product.

1. **Domain** — short blog posts for solo developers and homelabbers.
   → recorded as the workspace's purpose in `AGENTS.md`.
2. **Voice / style** — plain, direct, British spelling, one takeaway at the end.
   → `_config/voice.md`.
3. **Structure** — title, one-line angle, 3–4 sections.
   → `stages/01_outline/references/structure.md`.
4. **Length** — ~600 words.
5. **Review points** — after the outline (stage 01) and after the draft (stage 02).
